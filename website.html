Welcome to Moontree Cafe

Moontree Cafe has a Magnificent structure which is considered as the Luxury Cafe in the city [&] entire southern Maharashtra, Moontree cafe combines the warm hospitality of Kolhapur with the immaculate service of the Sayaji brand. In just a short span of time, Moontree Cafe has become one of the most important landmarks of South Maharashtra. Designed by the finest architects with 120 rooms, Sayaji Kolhapur succeeds in mesmerizing the onlooker. The plush rooms, world-class amenities, ambient fine-dining restaurants and grand banquets make it the destination to be for the esteemed guests of the city.
Kolhapur is home to the famous Mahalaxmi temple, situated in southern Maharashtra. The city has great traditional values which are linked to the history & diversity. The land being ruled by chatrapati shivaji & its home to their Royal descendants. The kolhapur is a hub for artisans who craft the traditional Leather sandals also known as paytans.
